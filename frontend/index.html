About

A tiny virtual pet you raise in your browser. An egg hatches after one minute into one of four friends — a pup, a kitty, a frog, or a bunny. Feed it, play with it, clean up, and keep it happy.

How to play:

  • ◀ A — move the cursor between icons
  • ● B — select / confirm
  • ▶ C — cancel / go back

No login. Your pet lives on the server, tied to a private ID on this device and your recovery code.

Reset

This abandons your current pet and lays a fresh egg. Your recovery code stays the same. Are you sure?
